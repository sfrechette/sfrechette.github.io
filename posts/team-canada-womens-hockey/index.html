Team Canada Women's Hockey - Some interesting numbers...

February 18, 2022 One minute read
Tableau
tableau • datafam • hockey • data visualisation

Yes, Canada Women’s Hockey Team has reclaimed gold! I had this idea at the beginning of the Beijing Olympic Winter Games to design, build a visual about the women’s national team. With source data from Hockey Canada and The Women’s Hockey Web here is what I came up with: some interesting numbers about Age (age at time of olympics), Shoots/Catches (left-handed, right-handed players), and Team composition (players by birth provinces).

To see the visualisation on Tableau Public -> Team Canada Women’s Hockey
